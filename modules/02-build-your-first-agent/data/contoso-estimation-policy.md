## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the preparation, review, and approval of all project cost estimates and tender submissions for Contoso Infrastructure and its operating companies.

---

## 2. Estimation Stages

| Stage | Purpose | Accuracy | When Used |
|-------|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Order of Magnitude** | Initial feasibility | ±30-50% | Expression of Interest, early planning |
| **Preliminary Estimate** | Budget setting | ±15-25% | Business case, funding approval |
| **Definitive Estimate** | Tender preparation | ±5-10% | Competitive tender submission |
| **Control Estimate** | Project baseline | ±5% | Post-award, pre-construction |

## 4. Estimation Process

### 4.1 Workflow

```
1. Tender Received
   ↓
2. Go/No-Go Decision (within 5 business days)
   ↓
3. Estimating Team Assigned
   ↓
4. Site Visit & Scope Review
   ↓
5. Quantity Take-Off (QTO)
   ↓
6. Rate Build-Up (labor + plant + materials + subcontractors)
   ↓
7. Preliminary Estimate Assembly
   ↓
8. Risk Assessment & Contingency
   ↓
9. Margin & Commercial Review
   ↓
10. Approval (per matrix above)
    ↓
11. Tender Submission
```

### 4.2 Go/No-Go Criteria

A tender opportunity must score ≥60% on the Go/No-Go assessment to proceed:

| Criterion | Weight | Score (1-5) |

## 5. Risk & Contingency

### 5.1 Contingency Guidelines

| Project Type | Minimum Contingency | Typical Range |
|-------------|--------------------:|:-------------:|
| Roads & highways | 3% | 3-8% |
| Bridges & structures | 5% | 5-12% |
| Tunnelling | 8% | 8-15% |
| Marine works | 7% | 7-12% |
| Building construction | 4% | 4-8% |
| Mining infrastructure | 5% | 5-10% |

### 5.2 Risk Categories

| Category | Examples | Mitigation Approach |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **Ground conditions** | Unexpected rock, contamination, water table | Geotechnical investigation, provisional sums |
| **Design development** | Incomplete design at tender stage | Design contingency allowance |
| **Market volatility** | Material price increases, labor shortages | Escalation provisions, fixed-price quotes |
| **Program risk** | Weather delays, access constraints | Program float, acceleration provisions |
| **Client risk** | Scope changes, payment delays | Contract conditions, payment terms |

## 7. Escalation

### 7.1 Price Escalation Allowance

For projects with construction duration > 12 months, include escalation based on:

| Cost Element | Escalation Index | Current Annual Rate |
|-------------|-----------------|:------------------:|
| Labor | Fair Work Commission annual increase | 3.5% |
| Concrete & cement | CPI + materials index | 4.2% |
| Steel | BIS steel price index | 5.8% |
| Fuel & energy | AIP terminal gate price | 3.0% |
