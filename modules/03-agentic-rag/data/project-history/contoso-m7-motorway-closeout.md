## Lessons Learned

### What Went Well
1. **Pavement works** — bulk purchasing agreement with quarry supplier saved 8% on aggregates
2. **Traffic management** — early engagement with TfNSW reduced stage changes by 15%
3. **Drainage** — precast elements reduced installation time by 25%

### What Could Improve
1. **Geotechnical risk** — deeper investigation needed for rock profiles; consider additional boreholes on future motorway projects
2. **Services relocation** — unknown Telstra assets discovered 8 months into construction; budget additional 20% contingency for services on brownfield corridors
3. **Steel supply chain** — 12-week lead time blowout due to port congestion; secure early orders for structural steel on future projects
4. **Subcontractor management** — earthworks sub went into administration mid-project; maintain backup subcontractor agreements for critical trades

### Recommendations for Future Projects
- Include minimum 15% rock contingency on any project with geotechnical uncertainty
- Mandate pre-construction services survey (DBYD + physical potholing) before earthworks commence
- Secure structural steel orders within 4 weeks of contract award
- Maintain panel of 3+ qualified earthworks subcontractors per region
